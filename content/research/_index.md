My research sits at the intersection of cybersecurity, public policy, and geopolitics. I'm interested in how we measure and model cyber risk at a systemic level — particularly for critical infrastructure and government networks — and how those findings translate into actionable policy.

I was selected as an **RSA Conference Security Scholar '24**, and my graduate research at UMD's School of Public Policy focused on attack surface quantification and strategic risk modelling for US infrastructure.
